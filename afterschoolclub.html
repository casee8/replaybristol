Our Story...

We just wanted to create a place that we would like to go to..

Replay was created by 2 crossing ways and creating a new dimension of entertainment. We`ve always had a passion for gaming so it wasn`t a what kind of business we would build if we had the chance.. and than the chance has arrived! When we got the to the premises -which others believe is curst as no business survived for more than a year before- we started our beautiful journey. First we`ve learnt how to work with wood to build and what is interior design, how to etc. Okay, this part wasn`t that good, mainly tears and sweat. When the place was ready, we built the social media sites and the website and started using our terrible marketing ideas. We have been market researching as a result of selecting social, party and family games. Our opening party was on the 16 June 2017 where only our friends turned up and than rarely anyone for weeks.. This time was our game of endurance where the object was to last as long as possible under a huge stress factor. We still remember the day - it was 15 degrees and - when customers started coming, staying and playing! So we did not give up instead we used our spare and started listening to our customers` ideas which has made a big impact. Since we opened we have met many great people and this is the biggest benefit of all. We to recommendations and constantly expand our selection.

We offer over 200 board-games as well as a table-tennis, table football, mini pool, dart-ball, and enough space to play Twister or under the disco light! The Replay project will be complete with an interactive escape room experience which is under progress at the moment.

We up-cycle! All our furniture and decorations made by us using recycling resources. We have made huge wooden tables to offer the perfect level of comfort for gaming.

We're proud to say that we locally source many of the items we sell; try our homemade vegan or homemade, We are also planning to implement a vegan/vegetarian menu in the next couple of months. Stay tuned!

We are delighted to bring this unique gaming experience to our local community and we welcome your input! We'll be increasing our choice of games and events, based on your recommendations!
